# Deep Learning

## Course Introduction

A systematic study of deep learning fundamentals and mainstream frameworks, including neural network basics, CNN, RNN, Transformer, generative models, and more.

## Learning Objectives

- Understand the basic principles of neural networks and backpropagation
- Master mainstream architectures such as CNN, RNN, and Transformer
- Become proficient with the PyTorch framework
- Be able to independently design and train deep learning models

## Reference Books

1. *Deep Learning* — Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio, Aaron Courville
2. *Dive into Deep Learning* — Li Mu et al.
